Ingredient Picker

The modal that opens from Add to pantry → pick an ingredient. A debounced search drives a flat list of dictionary tiles — a 44px category thumbnail, a title, mini-tags, and a chevron — with variant rows indented under their parent. When nothing matches, the search-off empty state offers to create the ingredient on the spot.
