Pantry Sections

Four colors organize the pantry into its top-level shelves. food reuses brandPrimary; bulk is the warm brown that doubles as lowStock; nonFood is a calm blue-gray; and leftover is a deep orange. Section colors are not re-tuned for the dark theme — they carry the same hues, shown live below.
