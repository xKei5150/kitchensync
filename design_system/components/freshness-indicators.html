Freshness Indicators

The small primitives that signal how close an item is to its expiry: a left-edge bar on every pantry tile, a status dot, an expiry badge, and a low-stock pill. All four read from one semantic set — fresh, expiringSoon, expired, unknown — derived from the item's expiry date by FreshnessHelper.
