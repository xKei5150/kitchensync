Section Tabs

The pantry section selector: a hairline-bordered bar holding four pills — Food, Bulk, Non-food, Leftovers. The active section fills with its --section-* color; the rest stay raised and quiet, tinting their icon to 70%. Selection cross-fades over 300ms on easeOutCubic.
