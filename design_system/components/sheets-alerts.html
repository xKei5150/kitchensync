Sheets & Alerts

Three feedback surfaces that confirm, warn, and recover. The destructive mark-as-waste sheet leans entirely on expired; the inline alert and snackbar carry the same semantic weight at smaller scales. Tints use color-mix to mirror Flutter's withValues(alpha:). The sheet shell, icon chip, and inline error match MarkAsWasteSheet today; the reason chips, the headlineMedium title, and the snackbar are proposed refinements.
