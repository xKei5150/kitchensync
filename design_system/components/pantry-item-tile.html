Pantry Item Tile

The workhorse row of the pantry list. A 4px freshness bar reads at a glance, a category-tinted thumbnail anchors the name, and the quantity is treated as a real stat. The left bar takes radius16 on its leading corners; the whole tile is surfaceRaised, radius16, with a 1px border.
